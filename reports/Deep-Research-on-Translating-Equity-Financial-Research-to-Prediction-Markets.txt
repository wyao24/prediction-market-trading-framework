### Translating ÔÇťretail vs institutionalÔÇŁ into prediction markets

## What changes or is absent in prediction markets

### Liquidity, ticks, and the ÔÇťmeaningÔÇŁ of a spread

Prediction markets often have both **thin participation** and **high relative frictions** because contracts are priced between 0 and 1.
